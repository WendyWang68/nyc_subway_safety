Opinion Board

Feel free to share your own experiences or concerns about the safety issue of NYC subway!


As a college student living in New York City, taking the train to commute to school campus is an unavoidable part of my life. I always want to explore the amazing city on the weekends with friends. But oftentimes due to my worries about the safety of the train, I postponed my plans. It is like an unpredictable adventure every time I leave my apartment. In addition, I always avoid getting home late at night by taking the train. I cannot feel secure having dinner at restaurants when the dark night starts to fall, which is supposed to be an enjoyable dinner time with my friends. I feel limited and vulnerable in my life when the necessary commuting way is not anymore safe. - Wendy Wang


I live in Brooklyn and I need to commute between NYU Manhattan and Brooklyn campus for classes. I usually take the school bus but it is relatively slow. The school bus is not always on-time. Taking an Uber is just so expensive for me. Subway is the fastest and most convenient way. It is impossible for me to avoid taking the subway. Commuting is a big problem for me now, and it even distracts my studies since I need to worry about my safety while commuting on the subway. I am mentally illed because of the subway safety issues. - Amber Li
