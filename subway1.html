Safety Tips

1. Wait behind the yellow line and stay away from the platform edge.

2. Keep your belongings in front of you.

3. Stay alert around your surroundings. Do not wear headphones and listen to loud music.

4. Avoid falling asleep when riding the subway.

5. Beaware of your back and avoid people approaching you from behind.

6. Trust your gut. If you ever feel uncomfortable near anyone, leave and go to a spot that you feel better.

7. If you spot something, please say something. Call 888-NYC-SAFE or 911 emergencies. Speak to an MTA worker or a police officer.

8. NYU security service is open 24/7. You can call 212-998-2222 to ask for help or report an incident.
